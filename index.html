About Me

Driven by the intersection of chemistry, data science, and AI, I focus on developing predictive models using graph-based deep learning to decode complex molecular data.

As a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Butts Research Group at the University of Bristol, I'm spearheading the commercialisation of IMPRESSION, my PhD project predicting NMR parameters via 3D Graph Transformer networks. This involves refining the AI model, building robust software (APIs, containerisation with Docker), and bridging the gap between academic research and practical application.

My PhD at the University of Bristol focused on the continual refinement of IMPRESSION and the development of a 3D Graph Transformer network, grounded in my background in computational chemistry.
